Transcript

In this video, we are going to talk about the effects and symptoms of hormone imbalances! Different types of imbalances can cause different symptoms, but there are a few that are more general. This includes: unexplained weight gain or loss, fatigue, unexplained changes to the skin, hair loss, digestive issues, heart rate changes, extreme fatigue, trouble adapting to temperature, and so much more. Not only are there physical effects of hormone imbalances, but many emotional and cognitive ones as well. Things like mood swings, constantly being annoyed, anxiety, brain fog, bad memory and concentration, insomnia, and headaches are common symptoms of hormone imbalances. Other common effects of hormone imbalances include irregular menstrual cycles, fertility issues, urinary issues, gastrointestinal dysfunction, muscle pains, and high cholesterol. Remember, these symptoms vary depending on the imbalance, and everyone experiences!

Key info

Symptoms can be physical (fatigue, weight changes, skin/hair changes) and also emotional/cognitive (mood swings, anxiety, brain fog, sleep issues).
