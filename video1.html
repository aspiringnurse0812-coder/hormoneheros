You may be asking yourself, What is a hormone imbalance? Why should I care? Hormones affect our entire bodies, influencing our mood, food processing, energy levels, growth, and much more. Pretty much anything you can think of in our bodies is influenced by hormones! When there is an imbalance, this means that the body has too much or too little of one or more hormones. So you can only imagine what could happen if there is an imbalance or improper amount of hormones and what that could do to our bodies. This is what we will discuss throughout the rest of our journey on our website. Have so much fun learning about hormones!
